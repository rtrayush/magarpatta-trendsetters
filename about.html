About Us

A club built on service, since day one.

The Rotaract Club of Magarpatta Trendsetters is a chartered member of Rotaract International, District 3131 — a community of young people turning ambition into action.

Mission

Develop leaders through service.

We exist to give young professionals and students in Magarpatta a structured, supportive way to serve their community while building real leadership and professional skills — long before a job title asks them to.

Vision

A generation that leads by serving.

We see a Pune where young people don't wait until they "have time" to give back — where service is built into how a career and a life are designed from the start.

Our Journey

How Trendsetters came to be

2017

Club Chartered

Rotaract Club of Magarpatta Trendsetters is officially chartered under Rotaract District 3131, with a founding batch of 18 members.

2019

First District Award

Recognised for outstanding Community Service projects at the District 3131 awards night.

2021

Going Digital

Shifted programming online through the pandemic — running virtual workshops and donation drives without missing a beat.

2024

Crossing 60 Members

Club membership crosses 60 active Rotaractors across colleges and early careers in Pune.

2026

STAR Summit Host

Co-hosted the District 3131 STAR Leadership Summit, welcoming over 500 Rotaractors from across the district.

Our Affiliation
